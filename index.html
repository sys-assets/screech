View and change your settings

The Settings page allows you to manage your account settings, update your privacy and security settings, and set your preferences for how frequently you're contacted by LinkedIn.

  • Account preferences: Manage your basic profile information for your LinkedIn experience.
  • Sign in & security: Manage the settings that keep your account secure.
  • Visibility: Control who can see your profile and activity.
